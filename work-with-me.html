02  ·  App & Community

Story Loom

Narrative OS for the Resistance

Story Loom is narrative infrastructure for people who are done building on extractive terms. It's for those ready to organize their lived stories, shadow work, and deepest convictions into resistance-minded work — and find the community of aligned builders doing the same.

Right for you if: you're building something post-capitalist and need narrative infrastructure that actually matches your values. Not another funnel. A narrative field that attracts your people.

What's inside

  • Story Journal for lived experience & ideological fuel
  • Story Bank — your narrative field, not your content calendar
  • Conviction-to-transformation messaging you own
  • Anti-extractive positioning & offer design
  • Community of aligned resistance-minded builders
  • The WEAVE Method: Wander → Excavate → Architect → Voice → Embody
Book a discovery call → Learn more

The WEAVE Method

Story Loom is built on five phases — Wander, Excavate, Architect, Voice, Embody — each designed to move you from raw narrative material to a living brand practice.

The Active Imagination differentiator

The Excavate phase uses Jungian Active Imagination — Jung's method for entering into dialogue with the unconscious — to surface narrative material that performance-based brand work will always miss.
