Where to Begin

The right place to start
depends on where you are.

Not everyone arrives at this work from the same place. Some people have been building for years and the language still hasn't caught up to the depth of what they're doing. Some are at a genuine threshold — something is ending, something is forming, and they need a container to think it through. Some just want to start the conversation and see what's there.

All of those are good places to start. Here's how to find yours.
