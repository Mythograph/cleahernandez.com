The Method
"Who looks outside, dreams;
who looks inside, awakes."

— Carl Jung

Jungian psychology gives us a map of the psyche that most brand work never consults. Carl Jung's insight was that the unconscious is not a dumping ground for what we don't want to know — it's a generative field of narrative material, symbolic imagery, and unlived possibility.

Jungian Narrative Coaching applies this insight to the practice of building a business or creative practice. We work with shadow — the parts of your story you've been taught to hide. We work with Active Imagination — Jung's method for entering into dialogue with unconscious content. We work with the archetypal patterns that organize how you understand your role and your work. And we translate everything we find into language — into a brand voice, a narrative system, a set of convictions you can actually build from.
