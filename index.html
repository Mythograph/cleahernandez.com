The Jungian approach
"The brand is not what you say it is.
It's what they say it is."

— Marty Neumeier, The Brand Gap

Jungian narrative coaching goes beneath the surface — past the polished pitch deck, the performance of success, and the internalized rules of a system you didn't design — to find the story that's already there, waiting to organize your life and your work.

01

Excavation

We go deep — into origin stories, shadow patterns, wounds, and wisdom — to find the narrative that's been there all along.

02

Articulation

We shape the raw material into language that resonates — a brand voice that sounds like you at your most clear and courageous.

03

Integration

We build systems so the story lives in your work — not just a document, but a practice that compounds over time.

04

Activation

The story becomes strategy — informing how you show up, what you offer, how you market, and who you attract.

About Cléa

I've spent 25 years finding the true story inside organizations — and the humans who lead them.

I'm a Jungian Narrative Coach, brand strategist, copywriter, grantwriter, creative director, communications leader, journalist, and author. The thread through all of it: I find the true story, and I make it impossible to ignore.

I founded Mythograph to tell brand stories that heal, instead of spin and extract. I'm now building Story Loom as narrative infrastructure for resistance-minded founders — because the systems we use to tell our stories either replicate harm or help dismantle it.

I work at the intersection of depth psychology, language strategy, and brand systems — because the most important stories are rarely the first ones people tell.
