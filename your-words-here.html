How it unfolds

Six weeks. One thread.

We move through the WEAVE method — from raw narrative material to a brand and website that holds the full weight of who you are and what you're building.

Wk 1–2

Wander & Excavate

We take honest inventory — origin stories, wounds, convictions, contradictions. Then we go deeper, past the polished version, into the narrative material that hasn't made it into your public work yet. Active Imagination practices. Shadow work. Custom guided meditations to surface what performance-based work always misses.

Wk 3–4

Architect & Voice

Raw material becomes structure. Your clear point of view, defined audience, set of convictions that organizes everything. Then we shape your brand voice: the language that sounds like you at your most clear and courageous. Between sessions, you speak your stories to Cléa via Voxer — she helps you capture them while they're alive.

Wk 5–6

Embody & Build

We build the systems and the site. Your Story Loom workspace, fully configured. Your messaging platform, complete. And your website — written in your voice, organized around your convictions, handed to you at the end of week six.
