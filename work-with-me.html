04  ·  The Ongoing Container

The inner work that becomes your work in the world.

Jungian Narrative Coaching is for the founder who wants a creative, generative space to do the deeper work that makes everything else more coherent: understanding the shadow patterns that show up in how you talk about your work, excavating the stories you've been avoiding, integrating the parts of your history that haven't yet become useful.

This is not therapy. It's not brand coaching in the conventional sense. It's the intersection of depth psychology and narrative strategy, held in a space designed for people who believe surface answers aren't enough.
